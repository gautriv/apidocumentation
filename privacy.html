Being Technical Writer Privacy Policy

Last updated on March 19, 2025

Introduction

This Privacy Policy outlines how I, Being Technical Writer, collect, use, and disclose your personal information. By accessing and using my website, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

Information I Collect

Personal Information

I do not collect any personal information from you directly. You may choose to provide me with personal information, such as your name and email address, when you contact me through my contact form or subscribe to my newsletter. I may also collect information from third-party sources, such as Google Analytics and Google Adsense. This information may include your IP address, browser type, device type, and referring website.

Non-Personal Information

I may collect non-personal information such as:

  • Browser type
  • IP address
  • Demographic information

How I Use Your Information

I do not use or process any user data personally. The non-personal information collected is used for:

  • Improve my website and services
  • Analyze website traffic through Google Analytics
  • Display personalized ads through Google AdSense
  • Utilize Google Search for improved search functionality
  • Respond to your inquiries and requests
  • Send you my newsletter
  • Provide you with personalized advertising

Third-Party Services

I use the following third-party services:

  • Google Analytics for website analytics (Google's privacy policy)
  • Google AdSense for displaying ads (Google's privacy policy)
  • Google Search for improved search functionality
  • Social media icons for sharing purposes
  • Disqus for comments (Disqus's privacy policy)
  • GitHub for content storage and publishing
  • Tinyletter for newsletters (Tinyletter's privacy policy)
  • BuyMeACoffee for voluntary support

Please review the privacy policies of these services for more information. Note that I have no access to or control over the privacy policies of these services.

Cookies

I do not use any cookies on my website. However, third-party services that I use, such as Google Analytics and Google Adsense, may use cookies to collect information about your use of my website. Please refer to the respective privacy policies mentioned above for details. Note that I have no access to or control over the cookies used by third-party advertisers.

External Links

My blog may contain links to external sites. I am not responsible for the privacy practices of these sites.

Your Consent

By using my website, you explicitly consent to my privacy policy.

Information Security

I implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your information.

Changes to Privacy Policy

I reserve the right to modify this privacy policy at any time. Changes will be effective immediately upon posting.

Open Source Content

My blog is open source, and all content is intended for public view and use.

Sharing Your Information

I will not share your personal information with third parties without your consent. However, I may share your information with third parties in the following circumstances:

  • To comply with legal requirements
  • To protect my rights and property
  • To protect the safety of my users

Your Choices

You can unsubscribe from my newsletter at any time by clicking on the "Unsubscribe" link in any newsletter email. You can also contact me to request that I delete your personal information.

Contact Information

If you have any questions regarding this privacy policy, you may use the Contact form.
